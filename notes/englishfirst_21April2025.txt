Listening Today 
Lesson Aim
By the end of this lesson, Ss will be able to use set phrases to speculate on the truth of a statement. 
Target Language
Set phrases of speculation:
Certain – That’s definitely true; I don’t buy it; I don’t believe it; That can’t be true; That must be true
Uncertain – That’s probably true; He seems confident about it; It seems very unlikely; It doesn’t look like it; It sounds like a possibility; It could be true
Pronunciation

I have ever met our president on the plain when I was working.
I once met our president on the plane while I was working.
I have ever met... when I was working." mixes present perfect (have met) and past simple (was).
Use past simple throughout → "I once met..." and "while I was working."
